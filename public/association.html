Association Voluntary Elderly

Previous Next

AVE is an association for social promotion, aimed primarily at the elderly. It is based in the multi-purpose Social Center in 991 Daphne Street, London. It was established in 1991 with the aim of promoting, using, implementing and designing social activities that improve the quality of life of its member and of all visitors.

Today it represents, on the municipal territory, a reality of aggregation and concrete socialization not only for members and users of services, but for all citizens.

Particularly, the association has a wide offer. Indeed, members can use some common spaces and services, such as the library and the bar, or join some of the proposed events, such as a healty walk at the park.
